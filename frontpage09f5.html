101 - Canaima Lagoon

Image: 

5 of us in Roberto's beat up old Cesna, the compass actually fell out of the dash whilst we were flying. After spending the whole day driving out of Canaima park yesterday we have to hire a plane to get to within striking distance of Angel Falls today. It was a superb flight across the countryside, rivers, settlements and the Cerro Bolivar Iron Mine, where they are mining the entire mountain! The lagoon looked like a film set, blue water, white sand, coconut palms...it had actually been used to shoot 'Last of the Mohicans'.
