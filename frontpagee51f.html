113 - Rancho Grande Cloud Forest - Henri Pittier National Park

Image: 

The oldest national park in Venezuela, the Henri Pittier National Park, opened in 1937, a full decade before we had such things in the UK. The park headquarters looks deserted and derelict, however balanced on the top floor are some Danish Biology students conducting a study. The building was originally designed as a retreat for Venezuelan dictator, Gomez. It was never finished and is now part of the Univesity of Caracas. It sits on the lowest pass across the Andes for hundreds of miles in either direction, and is therefor on an important bird migratory route.
